Collaboration with RTIK on LiDi

Relawan TIK KOMINFO is a group of tech enthusiasts volunteering to support Indonesia's ICT programs. They promote digital literacy, provide training, and collaborate with government and private sectors. Their goal is to expand internet access and ICT usage nationwide. Join as an ICT enthusiast to contribute to their mission.

The formation of the Digital Sector Guidance in Higher Education, in collaboration with the Volunteer ICT Academy, is organizing an event focusing on digital literacy. The event will cover the four pillars of digital literacy and include workshops on implementing ICT tools for education. Limited spots available, check schedule and register soon.

I was the online operator for the digital literacy event. As the online operator, my main responsibilities involve managing OBS, Zoom, and the PowerPoint presentations for the speakers. With my knowledge and expertise in operating OBS and Zoom, as well as my videography skills, I ensure a seamless virtual experience for all participants. I have previous experience as a camera operator in the BISMAPATI event, which has given me valuable insights into event operations. Although I don't directly engage with participants during the event, I provide essential technical support and contribute behind the scenes to enhance the overall event quality. While I may not provide specific resources, my proficiency in managing the online platforms plays a crucial role in promoting digital literacy. Through my role as the online operator, my aim is to make the event more efficient and to maximize its overall impact.
